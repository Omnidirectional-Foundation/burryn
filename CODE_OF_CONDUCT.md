# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

You may communicate in **English or Chinese** — either is fine.

你可以使用**英文或中文**交流，任选其一。

## Standards / 准则

This is a small open-source hobby project. The rules are simple:

这是一个小型开源兴趣项目，规则很简单：

- **Be respectful.** Treat others as you would want to be treated.

  **保持尊重。** 以你希望被对待的方式对待他人。
- **Be constructive.** Critique ideas and code, not people.

  **保持建设性。** 对事不对人，批评想法和代码而非个人。
- **Stay on topic.** Keep issues and discussions relevant to the project.

  **不要跑题。** issue 和讨论请与项目相关。
- **No spam, harassment, or off-topic promotion.**

  **禁止灌水、骚扰或无关推广。**

## Not tolerated / 不被容忍的行为

- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory language.

  人身攻击、侮辱或歧视性言论。
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.

  任何形式的骚扰，无论公开或私下。
- Posting others' private information without consent.

  未经同意发布他人隐私信息。

## Enforcement / 执行

The maintainer may edit, lock, or delete any content, and may block any
contributor, that violates these standards — at their own discretion and
without obligation to explain.

维护者可酌情编辑、锁定或删除任何违反上述准则的内容，并可封禁任何相关贡献者，
无需作出解释。

> As with everything else in this hobby project, enforcement is best-effort and
> response is not guaranteed.
>
> 与本兴趣项目的其他方面一样，准则执行为尽力而为，不承诺响应。
